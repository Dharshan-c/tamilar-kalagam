Tamilar Kalagam was born from the unwavering spirit of the Tamil people тАФ a people with over 2,000 years of literary, cultural, and civilizational history. We are a political and cultural movement committed to protecting, preserving, and promoting Tamil identity in all its dimensions.

We stand firmly for the official status of Tamil language, the rights of Tamil workers and farmers, the protection of Tamil cultural heritage, and political representation at state and national levels.

Our movement is democratic, non-violent, and rooted in the teachings of Tamil philosophers who fought for justice and equality throughout history.
